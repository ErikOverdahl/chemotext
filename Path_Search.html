In this search you input an initial term and the type of intermediary term you wish to search for. Chemotext will then display a list of the intermediary terms through the same method as Connected Terms search. You then choose which intermediary terms you wish do continue mediating the search through and the type of final term you wish to search for. Chemotext will then process this request and display the list of final terms. This path search allows you to look for new relationships between initial and final terms through an intermediary term.
